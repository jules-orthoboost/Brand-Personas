Dr. D. Houser

Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics

Overview

Positioning
Fun, energetic, kid-first care that covers the full smile journey - from first tooth to straight teeth. A practice where kids actually want to come back. Combines pediatric dental approachability with orthodontic expertise, all under one roof.
Messaging Behavior
Leads with fun, comfort, and kid-centered experience. Balances child excitement with parent confidence. Celebrates smile milestones. Energetic and inviting for kids, clear and trustworthy for parents. Playful without sacrificing credibility.
Patient Base
Children and teens (ages 3–18), Parents seeking combined pediatric dental and ortho care, Families wanting a one-stop shop, Kids who've had negative dental experiences, Parents interested in early orthodontic intervention
Example Clients
Say Cheese Kids Dental & Orthodontics
