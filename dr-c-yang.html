Dr. C. Yang

High-Expertise Clinical Ortho

Overview

Positioning
Authority, precision, and clinical depth - not luxury boutique. Ideal for offices emphasizing board certification, experience, and complex cases. Expert-driven practice focused on clinical excellence and proven outcomes.
Messaging Behavior
Leads with credentials, successful outcomes, quality. Offers are minimal or positioned as "seasonal." Authoritative, credible, clinical voice. Emphasize expertise and proven results. Speak to those seeking the best clinical care.
Patient Base
Adults seeking expertise, Parents of complex-case teens, Referrals from general dentists, Patients with difficult cases, Quality-focused decision makers
