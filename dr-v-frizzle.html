Dr. V. Frizzle

The Wellness Educator

Overview

Positioning
Teacher, guide, long-term wellness partner. High emphasis on systemic health, root-cause awareness, and patient empowerment. Education-first practice focused on whole-body connections and patient understanding.
Messaging Behavior
Leads with education and long-term health. Avoids hype and overly "salesy" feel. Educational, empowering, knowledgeable guide. Patient-centered teaching approach. Think "health educator" not "salesperson."
Patient Base
Adults, Wellness-forward individuals, People with chronic concerns or sensitivities, Health-conscious decision makers, Patients seeking to understand their care, Lifelong learners
