Overview

Positioning
Practical, straightforward, cost-conscious. Focuses heavily on affordability, convenience, and accessibility.

Leads with price, financing, and convenience. "Affordable," "Flexible," "Hassle-Free." Avoids overly clinical or luxurious language.
Patient Base
